Banking for the Elderly

After a long and interesting talk with my grand-father in law, it became apparent that the rapid speed of changing technogies and implementation of new designs, layouts, etc on the web, has had some unwanted (and possibly unknown!?) side-effects for web users, particularly among the older generations. Online Banking processes which have been memorised through repeated use over a number of years, can be changed overnight and leave those with lessening cognitive abilities somewhat lost and incapable to perform some of the most basic operations with their critical online banking functions; for example moving money and requesting a replacement card/ pin. With the current trend of banks 'pushing' their users to use online banking, how can we combat such problems compounded by reduced memory function, ability to learn and limited patience for the current rate of change?
